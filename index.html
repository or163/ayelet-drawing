Meet Ayelet Hashachar

Art, music, literature, and a soul full of colors

A Soul Full of Creativity

Ayelet Hashachar is a passionate creative spirit with a deep love for art and drawing. Her canvases overflow with bold colors and raw emotion — from vibrant pop-art portraits of rock legends like Lennon, Cobain, and Hendrix, to delicate floral wildlife illustrations and abstract mixed-media textures. Every brushstroke carries feeling.

Beyond the canvas, Ayelet Hashachar is captivated by music — rock, soul, and everything that moves the heart. But above all, she is deeply in love with literature. Books and words are woven into her art and her world, as seen in her newspaper collage works where text becomes texture, and stories become visual poetry.

It is this unique blend of visual art, musical soul, and literary depth that makes Ayelet Hashachar's work so layered, so emotional, and so unmistakably hers.
